About

In 1933, founder Sol Koffler invests his life savings into a luggage company in Providence, Rhode Island. He wants to turn his dream into reality. Heeding the call for lighter luggage sparked by increased air travel, American Tourister introduces Hi-Taper luggage that is lighter and easier to pack. In 1946, we launch our first national advertising campaign featuring luxurious interiors, fashionable colours and durable construction.
